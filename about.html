Serving Healthcare Since 2010

At Timon Tender Care Pharmaceuticals Private Limited, we have been delivering trusted pharmaceutical solutions and medicine supply for over a decade. Established in 2010 and based in Chennai, our commitment to product quality, reliability, and healthcare excellence has made us a preferred pharmaceutical distributor for hospitals, clinics, pharmacies, and healthcare professionals across India.

From research-based generic medicines to branded formulations, we ensure that every product meets the highest standards of safety, efficacy, and affordability. With a dedicated team and a strong distribution network, we continue to support healthcare delivery—serving hospitals, medical facilities, and independent pharmacies. Timon Tender Care Pharmaceuticals Private Limited is committed to making quality medicines accessible to all.
